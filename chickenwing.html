Description

Breaded, pan-fried chicken wings.

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs chicken wings
  • all-purpose flour
  • Black pepper, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet with oil
  2. Combine flour with spices, whisk and sit aside
  3. Toss chicken wings in egg yoke, followed by flour
  4. Allow wings to fry in oil till brown
