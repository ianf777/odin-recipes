Description

Classic cheeseburger with lettuce, onion, and tomato

Ingredients

  • Burger Buns
  • American Cheese
  • 1/4 lb beef patty
  • Lettuce
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Heat a skiller, cook beef patty in it till browned on both sides
  2. Take burger bun, place beef patty on the burger bun
  3. Layer cheese, lettuce, and onion respectively
  4. Finish with condiments of choice, then place bun on top
