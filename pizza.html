Pizza

HOME

Description

A small, plain, cheese pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 420
  2. Massage pizza dough into a circle
  3. Cover pizza dough with tomato sauce everywhere besides crust
  4. Layer with Mozzarella cheese
  5. Add toppings (if any)
  6. Slide into oven and allow it to bake untill cheese becomes brown
